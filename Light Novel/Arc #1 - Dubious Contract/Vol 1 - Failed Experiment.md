1039 A.G Paulin 14th, Gannday at about Sunnilow. Another beautiful average summer morning, with a humid wind carrying hints of salty, fishy air, contrasting with a warming sun tickling the skin. *He’s crankier than usual.* A ponderous apprentice by the name of Kibe moved his eyes around the unusually large specimen of bagarisa, while avoiding glancing back at the source of the accusing voice. “You’re the one who asked me to bring the most powerful thing around.”  
Tightly clenching his teeth, Gabiro made a nerve pop up in his forehead. He seemed to hold back for what seemed an instant, but soon after he extended his hand towards the dead bagarisa, and exploded verbally. “Were you smoking Sanbi shit? Kibe blinked.

The comforting warmth, the natural forest aroma in the air, plus the company of people he liked meant the world to him. He relaxed his face, dipping further into the hot water with a vacant mind.
