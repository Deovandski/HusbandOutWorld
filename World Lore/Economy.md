# \--Economy--

# **Isekai History**

* Economical activities stem from Fauna, flora, & beasts, and the goods/services derived from them. Economic activity is divided into 4 regions, where Comparative advantage determines which goods can be exported from one region to another.

# **Comparative Advantage**

* The ability of members of an economical region to carry out a particular economic activity more efficiently than members of another economical region  
* \*D.T.C must be accounted for when claiming in favor of or against.

# **Economical Regions**

* ## **Freeish Region**

  * This region consists of Forway, Triangle, Deltacross, Freeish, Blood, Sweetlake, Sundeath, and any other village within its shared boundaries.  
  * Members of this region focus on exporting a ton of flora and fauna.

* ## **Gandew Region**

  * This region consists of Forway, Flatland, Gandew, Sundeath, and any other village within its shared boundaries.  
  * Members of this region focus on exporting minerals, Wooly, and chitin.

* ## **Sanbi Region**

  * This region consists of Forway, Sanbi, Waterfall, Triangle, Tastyleaf, Endlessfog, and any other village within its shared boundaries.  
  * Members of this region focus on exporting Sanbi, fish-related products, etc.

* ## **Tolerideme Region**

  * This region consists of Forway, Tolerideme, Waterfall, Tastyleaf, Wingarest, and any other village within its shared boundaries.  
  * Members of this region focus on exporting Domar Carbey, Wood People, and Wingas.

# **Sapphire**

* **Hardness**  
  * 9  
* **History**  
  * Mined in Flatland City to Blood City within the mountains at great depths (\~1km)  
  * Slightly bigger than a fingernail. \~2cm diameter.  
* **Value**  
  * **Direct Conversions**  
    * 1 Sapphire \= 10 Aquamarine  
    * 1 Sapphire \= 100 Amethyst  
    * 1 Sapphire \= 5,000 Pearls  
  * **Indirect Conversions**  
    * Buy a fully grown tamed Egua  
    * Buy properties  
    * Slavery contracts.  
    * Buy guilds  
    * Buy specialized education.  
    * Tier IX+ Soul Energy

# **Aquamarine**

* **Hardness**   
  * 7.5-8  
* **History**  
  * Mined in Flatland City to Blood City within the mountains at great depths (\~1km)  
  * Slightly bigger than a fingernail. \~2cm diameter.  
* **Value**  
  * **Direct Conversions**  
    * 1 Aquamarine \= 10 Amethyst  
    * 1 Aquamarine \= 500 Pearls  
  * **Indirect Conversions**  
    * Custom-crafted armor/weapons, or good gear in general.  
    * Buy child tamed Eguas  
    * Buy adult tamed Big Wingas.  
    * Well protected/guarded travel.  
    * Fancier hotels.  
    * Some people’s entire life savings.  
    * Buy fundamental education.  
    * Fancy clothing, usually with gold or precious metals details  
    * Tier VI \~ VIII Soul Energy.

# **Amethyst**

* **Hardness**   
  * 7  
* **History**  
  * Mined in Flatland City to Blood City within the mountains at great depths (\~1 km)  
  * Slightly bigger than a fingernail. \~2 cm diameter.  
* **Value**  
  * **Direct Conversions**  
    * 1 Amethyst \= 50 Pearls  
  * **Indirect Conversions**  
    * 1 Nari Stone.  
    * Common household Items such as a table, Fost lamps, etc.  
    * Fancier foods/Drinks.  
    * Average armor/weapons.  
    * Better Travel experience.  
    * Average lodging that often offers breakfast.  
    * Buy new, cheap clothing or some more refined clothing.  
    * Maps are usually priced at this rate for Aquamarine for more complex ones.  
    * Most Nari Gels, if desiring higher effect tiers.  
    * Tier IV \~ VI Soul Energy  
    * Sigil Rings  
    * Winga companion.

# **Pearl**

* **Hardness**   
  * 2.5 \- 4.5  
* **History**  
  * Originally acquired by fishermen from Sanbi, but became a task dedicated to Cruros when they showed up since it was close to free labor.  
  * Slightly bigger than a fingernail. \~2 cm diameter.  
* **Value**  
  * **Direct Conversions**  
    * Lowest component of the currency.  See other ball types.  
  * **Indirect Conversions**  
    * Common Food & beer.  
    * Lodging for a day in a hostel or a cheap hotel.  
    * Some bargain/cheap armor/weapons.  
    * Hay for a horse.  
    * Travel by wagon, but not as comfortable or protected.  
    * Utensils.  
    * Buy used clothing, sometimes with wear and tear to some very cheap clothing.  
    * Most Nari Gels, if seeking weak-effect tiers.  
    * Tier I \~ III Soul Energy  
    * Paper, ink, etc.
