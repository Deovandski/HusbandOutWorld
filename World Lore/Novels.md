# **From Blood Bag To Cattle**

* **Characters**  
  * None  
* **Approximated Age**  
  * \~2,100 B.G. \- \~1,650 B.G.  
* **Isekai History**  
  * It all began when humans awakened in the islands of the Burhaies plains. The green trees and overall peaceful nature contrasted with the pink/red trees that seemed to watch over them from all around.   
  * A paradise with beautiful overlords who taught and brought them food of all kinds soon turned into a brutal reality. They were walking food rations for the Buhraies. Their blood tasted the most sweet when they were the most fearful for their lives. They began to run around like wild game, but some of them figured out that the Wood people, as they would call the pink/red trees, only looked frightening as they did nothing (Because they were chained by Buhraies with Fost Stone). However, Buhraies seemed to attack at random, almost as if enjoying keeping humans on edge.  
  * Some humans used dirt and leaves to reduce their profile and began spreading out, living in the forests as they looked for ways to escape. That’s when they discovered the East dip. A cliff with failures which they could use to reduce how far they would have to climb down, and the steepness of the whole thing.  
  * Over a few years, they gradually weakened the cliff, causing it to collapse. With that, humans were freed from Buhraies’s fangs and into the hungry mouth of the Lishies.

* **Characters**  
  * Freeish Paulin

  * \~640 A.G.  
* **Isekai History**  
  * In 650 A.G., the courtroom scientists of Gandew, Tolerideme, & Sanbi Kingdoms meet on this hidden base to discuss an overthrow plan.  
    * Gandew: Asa the Powerful  
    * Tolerideme: Adrian From North Tolerideme  
    * Sanbi: Bijura Moacir  
    * They slowly conditioned the populace to accept what they were trying to do in order to instigate revolts.  
      * “The stones were controlling the people”  
    * The kingdoms gradually lost their grip on the populace until they could no longer go to war with one another. At that point, the rebellion truly started and toppled all three kingdoms.  
      * Gandew King was executed in front of everyone. He had a calm, relieved demeanor despite what had happened to him. Only a few people know of his true actions.  
      * Tolerideme King was starved to death  
      * Sanbi King was humiliated in public by being spanked by multiple people. He was then dropped alive into the ocean.  
  * The overthrow plan succeeded, and the courtroom scientist became the new king.  
  * They crafted the Prosperity Treaty and Forway law to ensure that no more wars would occur between them over greed.  
  * They created a common currency, formalized a calendar that began in the year Ganyrami fell, and developed other tools to bring everyone on the same page and make trading easier.   
  * Embassies were founded soon after, and all ruins/major cities became property of Forway.  
    * Sundeath tried to stay out of everything, but failed due to the sheer pressure from Forway. In exchange, they have Pharaohs that serve as city leaders.
