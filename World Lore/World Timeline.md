# **\--Finaies--**

## **The Evolution Time (Unknown \- \~10,000 B.G.)**

* **The Unknown Period (Unknown \- \~100,000 B.G.)**  
  * What is now known about the Finaies came about during this period—the ability to move about, reproduce sexually, Soul Energy abilities, etc.  
  * Their habitat was north of Hifuna on the planet's northern continent.  
* **The Landscaping Period (\~100,000 B.G \- \~50,000 B.G.)**  
  * With self-awareness and the ability to move about, they began spreading south, conquering the entire continent and shaping the land to resemble the north.  
* **The Revolution Period (\~50,000 B.G \- \~10,000 B.G.)**  
  * As different groups were exposed to different environments on the southern continent, distinct specialization groups began to form as the land they inhabited shaped them.  
  * From such specializations, three new races formed: Fuhraies, Lishies, and Buhraies. They quickly spread and began clashing with one another for territory.

## **The Devolution Time (\~10,000 B.G. \- Current)**

* **The Cleansing Period (\~10,000 B.G \- \~5,215 B.G.)**  
  * The Finaies back in the north grew aware of a disturbance of the natural order of the world—or at least what they deemed natural. They began sending scouts, then armies, but to no avail. Their offspring's specialization triumphed over their generalistic nature.  
* **The Isolation Period (\~5,215 B.G \- \~3,000 B.G.)**  
  * \~5,215 B.G. Given the extent of their losses, they decided to sacrifice themselves to create a barrier between the northern and southern continents, now known as Hifuna.  
    * They spent most of their army creating a curtain of meteors with radiation to create a barrier.  
  * Later on, they formed a pact with Flingas to observe the south and plan a comeback.  
  * They made several attempts at invading through the sea, but they were all massacred.  
    * \~3,150 B.G. The Great Sea war ended in a massive loss for them, but they at least managed to destroy part of the Buhraies Plains East Dip.  
* **Hifuna Conquer Period (\~3,000 B.G \- \~2,500 B.G.)**  
  * Their society spent so much time planning another comeback that, by the end, they became incapable of resolving their own mess—Hifuna.  
    * They mounted the largest army they could muster, but they suffered massive losses as they traversed Hifuna.  
  * \~2,650 B.G. The Buhraies mounted a secret assault through the desert, but the desert killed off most of them. Upon reaching the Fuhraies, the army left over was easily massacred.  
* **The Replanning Period (\~2,500 B.G \- Current)**  
  * They’ve grown conservative in their plans—so much so that they are still planning, or rather, watching what happens in the south and discussing amongst themselves as if it were a TV Show.

# **\--Fuhraies--**

## **Cold Growth Time (\~20,000 B.G. \- \~5,215 B.G.)**

* **Up the Mountain Period (\~20,000 B.G \- \~10,000 B.G.)**  
  * The Finaies near the snow mountains began to acclimate to the colder regions located in the higher altitudes. Over time, they began to move further towards the peaks. As they did, their body began to change—their biological rhythm slowed down, and they grew calmer, almost as if they were depressed.  
* **Gridlock Period (\~10,000 B.G \- \~5,215 B.G.)**  
  * Now the Fuhraies had no difficulty fending off the Finaies from their homes, but they also were stuck in a battling deadlock as they simply couldn’t leave the cold regions anymore, as anything above 0º was unbearable for them.  
  * \~5,215 B.G. Hifuna was created, and with that, peace came to the Fuhraies.

## **Cold Defense Time (\~5,215 B.G. \- \~2,100 B.G.)**

* **Windless Snow Period (\~5,215 B.G \- \~3,500 B.G.)**  
  * A period of peace ensued where resources were plentiful, and unoccupied snowy terrain was visible as far as the eye could see.  
    * Due to the other two races having too much land to grow.  
* **Blue Snow Period (\~3,500 B.G \- \~3,000 B.G.)**  
  * Buhraies decided to test everything they knew about subjugation on Fuhraies, but it did not work. They began fighting one another. Or rather, Fuhraies kept defending themselves against the weak attacks of Buhraies, since they were not accustomed to such cold weather and altitude due to their fleshy nature.  
  * Their attempts at negotiation never worked.  
  * \~3,150 B.G. Finaies invaded through the sea, but Fuhraies were too far away to have been impacted by them, as Buhraies and Lishies killed everyone.  
* **Red Snow Period (\~3,000 B.G \- \~2,100 B.G.)**  
  * Lishins began fighting, or rather, bullying the Fuhraies as well, since the battle with the Buhraies was a stalemate. However, this battle also ended in a stalemate.  
    * Negotiations never went anywhere either.  
  * Some of the Fuhraies fled old Fuhrair north to the biggest mountain to create Fuhrair, as they were sick of the skirmishes.  
  * \~2,650 B.G. The Buhraies mounted a secret assault through the desert, but the desert took care of most of them, leaving the Fuhraies to easily defend themselves.  
  * The eternal skirmishes slowed, and their intensity decreased as well. Fuhraies assumed they had finally won.

## **Cold Fall Time (\~2,100 B.G. \- \~500 B.G.)**

* **The Calm Period (\~2,100 B.G \- \~600 B.G.)**  
  * The endless skirmishes between Fuhraies & Buhraies/Lishies finally ended, ushering in a period of calm.  
  * They shored up their defenses as much as they could, but over time their battle fangs dulled.  
* **The Naked Bruntos Period (\~600 B.G \- \~500 B.G.)**  
  * Seeing the rageful nature of Naked Bruntos, and how the other two races were quickly falling, the rest of their civilization fled into Fuhrair, and once again beefed up their defense as much as they could, especially in an attempt to hide from the Naked Bruntos.  
* **The Pact (\~500 B.G.)**  
  * Seeing how voracious humans were in their attacks, the Fuhraies agreed to a pact with the Buhraies, and thus their story began intertwined with theirs.  
  * The Last Breath Time for continuation.

    

# **\--Lishies--**

## **Toughening up Time (\~15,000 B.G. \- \~4,500 B.G.)**

* **Muscle Development Period (\~15,000 B.G \- \~10,000 B.G.)**  
  * The Finaies in the deep forest developed a taste for meat, and so their bodies started to change slightly, then a lot, as they became excellent hunters. The females preferred beefy, fleshy men, so they steered evolution towards the non-existence of the Finaies interface.  
  * Lishies became a new species.  
* **Eat the Land Period (\~10,000 B.G \- \~4,500 B.G.)**  
  * They developed a tribal and territorial nature, which forced them to expand horizontally quickly to prevent excessive internal conflict. In doing so, they began clashing with the Buhraies so often that they became acquainted with the blood Soul Energy and found ways to fight it.  
  * \~6,000 B.G.\~5,000 B.G. That was the moment Buhraies created their plains and separated themselves. They kept trying to find ways to reach them for payback, but were unsuccessful due to the lack of a Soul Energy interface.  
  * \~5,215 B.G. Finaies Retreated.  
  * The Lishies spread through the forest, particularly towards the South-West, due to the abundance of available food. Their population grew denser and even tamer in places with enough food and strong tribal leadership.

## **Clashing Time (\~4,500 B.G. \- \~0 A.G.)**

* **Resisting Subjugation Period (\~4,500 B.G \- \~3,500 B.G.)**  
  * The lishies once again clashed with the Buhraies and discovered how to use the Buhraies' bodies as a Soul Energy interface to fly into the Buhraies' plains, since climbing was not enough due to the sheer height. Carving a path always leads to a skirmish, which would simply take too long.  
  * They often had constant skirmishes and swoops to acquire more bodies to mount an all-out war.  
  * \~3,575 B.G. Lishies launched a war against Buhraies, wiping each other's populace as the battle raged on.  
* **Overtaking Subjugation Period (\~3,500 B.G \- \~2,100 B.G.)**  
  * Lishies realized the Buhraies were attempting to subjugate the Fuhraies, so they tried to kill them off first to prevent it. They seemed to be stuck in a fruitless battle when suddenly they realized they had lost Endlessfog Island.  
  * \~3,150 B.G. Finaies launched a massive strike from the sea, but Lishies were not as affected as Buhraies, as most Lishies lived down south.  
  * \~2,650 B.G. Buhraies were terraforming Endlessfog right under their noses, while they seemed distracted with preparing to fight back the Finaies if they were to be a powerful menace this time.  
  * Not exactly as dumb as Buhraies thought they were, they began playing 4D chess with them as they themselves started to fake battling with Buhraies while planning a takeover of the island.  
  * \~2,100 B.G. Lishies found a way to retrieve the island back, and began using the Bruntos as a peculiar food source. However, the fog meant they could not eat them all, so the island was mostly abandoned after they had their fill.  
* **Interfighting Period (\~2,100 B.G \- \~1,100 B.G.)**  
  * With the Buhraies and Fuhraies in the defensive, the Lishies began to power-struggle among themselves, to the point where several wars broke out between tribes.  
  * Some attempted to figure out why Buhraies was on the defensive, but none of their efforts yielded any useful findings.  
  * Some Lishies even began to forget that Buhraies & Fuhraies existed.  
  * \~1650 B.G. Naked Bruntos started to appear, but Lishies treated them just like any other animal around—as if they would hunt them down for dinner.  
    * They also tasted great, so they began to create human herds and fatten them up like cattle. Unaware of how intelligent they were, Lishies allowed Humans to grow and prosper under their noses, as often as they would plan their escape.  
* **Wild Bruntos Extermination Period (\~1,100 B.G \- \~900 B.G.)**  
  * Several Lishin tribes began a mission of eating every Wild Brunto in sight as they began realizing how many of these “animals” were roaming around, but it seemed like it was too late to find every single one. However, they were making steady progress until humans fought back in ways other than physical...  
* **The Path of No Return Period (\~900 B.G \- \~500 B.G.)**  
  * \~855 B.G. Lishin and other tribes began attacking the Buhraies, thinking it was a prime time to do so, but it was false information from the humans.  
  * \~805 B.G. A massive civil war broke out in Aldemeri as it burned for days, all instigated by Humans.  
  * Some residents, who were less inclined toward war, fled to other cities (or the flatland) and began trying to put a stop to another civil war.  
    * \~805 B.G. Several Lishies fled into what is now Flatland and entered into a pact with Buhraies after a battle that dwindled numbers on both sides.  
  * Lishies were steadily losing ground from the Naked Brunton's invasion South and East of the Buhraies Plains.  
  * \~525 B.G. A massive war was declared between Ganyrami and Lishin, also provoked by humans after failing to promote an internal civil war.  
    * Lishin Fell.  
    * Humans picked over the remains.  
* **Fall From Grace Period (\~500 B.G \- \~0 A.G.)**  
  * Humans worked their way through countless tribes, which led to the biggest ancient species city, Ganyrami, being the last defensive position of this species.  
  * Amid chaos, infighting, and disorganization, humans ultimately killed off the entire city, culminating in the historic Fall of Ganyrami.  
  * The Lishin blood continued in the history of The Last Breath Time.  
  * Some Lishies might have survived southwest in the woodland.

# **\--Buhraies--**

## **Rise of the Flesh Time (\~15,000 B.G. \- \~5,000 B.G.)**

* **Blood Feast Period (\~15,000 B.G \- \~10,000 B.G.)**  
  * The Finaies near what is now Tolerideme began to feast on fauna/beasts for blood & bone marrow. The variety of blood consumed triggered a mutation of their bodies. In other words, the creation of Buhraies.  
* **Blood & Soul Energy Period (\~10,000 B.G \- \~6,000 B.G.)**  
  * The Buhraies developed their own interface with blood. Its hunger for more power led it to clash with the Finnaies from the north and the Lishins from the South at the same time.  
  * The constant battling led them to further flesh out their capabilities. At the same time, Lishies began to grow resistant to it, resulting in a stalemate.  
* **Landscaping Period (\~6,000 B.G \- \~5,000 B.G.)**  
  * With a looming threat of Lishies' overwhelming numbers, Buhraies began terraforming a large section of the plains off the ground using Finaies' bodies ground into the ground to create a safe zone from the Lishies called Buhraies Plains.  
    * An average 2km cliff in the air that does not fall apart through erosion.  
    * Fost stones were formed during the process due to interference from Soul Energy deep within the land. Buhraies began using it as a light source, which Lishies eventually learned about, but its use was not widespread, as making fire pits was easier for them.  
  * Additional earth was used from where Lukewarm Buhraies Lake now exists. They created an underground structure that pipes the water into Wingarest.  
  * \~5,215 B.G. At this point, Finaies retreated after failing to bring down the Buhraies Plains and created Hifuna.

## **Blood Research Time (\~5,000 B.G. \- \~2,100 B.G.)**

* **First Subjugation Period (\~5,000 B.G \- \~4,500 B.G.)**  
  * With the Finaies out of the picture, and the Lishies out of reach, their society began to flourish, and they began research on how to subjugate the other two races after being able to modify and control Wingas and Yabuca Trees into Big Wingas and Wood People and bend them to their will.  
    * Wingarest became the central place for Big Wingas to live in.  
    * Wood People were created by altering the souls of Yabuca trees. Then, the Wood People were confined to small forests throughout the Buhraies plains as they produced food and lumber in a controlled manner.  
  * Their goal: Subjugate and experiment on the Finaies in the north, plus fix and terraform Hifuna.  
* **Lishies Subjugation Period (\~4,500 B.G \- \~3,500 B.G.)**  
  * They attempted to subjugate the Lishies, but not only did it fail, but it also led them to discover how to climb into the Buhraies Plains. Albeit with difficulty in combating them, the peaceful life on the plains was no more. They began to research how to keep Lishies at bay while they skirmished nearly every week.  
  * \~3,575 B.G. Lishies launched a war against Buhraies, wiping each other's populace as the battle raged on.  
* **Fuhraies Subjugation Period (\~3,500 B.G \- \~3,000 B.G.)**  
  * Frustrated that the subjugation of the Fuhraies had gone nowhere, the Buhraies began targeting the Fuhraies for subjugation, but that only led to fighting. The Lishies decided to attack as well, since they did not know which power they were seeking to turn the tide.  
  * \~3,150 B.G. Finaies launched their biggest strike from the sea  
    * During the invasion, the Buhraies Plains East Dip suffered the greatest loss of land. Repairs were performed, but the generation that performed them was not the same as the one that created it, so their ratios and practices did not align with the previous ones.  
* **Fake Battle Period (\~3,000 B.G \- \~2,100 B.G.)**  
  * Realizing that Lishies were focusing on the Fuhraies, Buhraies kept faking an eternal skirmish with the Fuhraies to keep the Lishies away from their true plot: Terraforming what is now Endlessfog, and experimenting with summoning species.  
    * Bruntos came about at that time.  
  * \~2,650 B.G. The Buhraies did not care about the failed Finaies invasion of the Fuhraies.  
  * \~2,450 B.G Buhraies discovered the Walking Heart stones, which are irradiated rocks mixed in Hifuna.  
  * \~2,100 B.G., they ultimately lost the island to the Lishies after the Lishies figured out a way to deal with the fog, as the Buhraies were no longer as focused on research on that island.

## **Naked Bruntos Time (\~2,100 B.G. \- \~855 B.C.)**

* **Bloodsport Period (\~2,100 B.G \- \~1,500 B.G.)**  
  * Licking their sore wounds (mostly emotional) from losing endlessfog island after thinking the Lishies were too dumb to plan something, they stopped all battles and focused on research.  
  * That’s when Naked Bruntos (Humans) came about. In an attempt to access their war skills, they discovered a hunting sport, especially how tasty their blood was when they were frightened and running for their lives.  
    * Some discovered that it was also tasty when some naked bruntos were extremely lustful for their tiny beauty.  
    * This led to tons of research, and the blood mixing of Buhraies into Humans.  
  * During this time, they discovered the power of souls and Walking Heart stones and created an endless army from them.  
    * One of the humans who was reborn was really good at mineralogy, so his knowledge was extracted, and with it, English names for minerals stuck. Gold, Iron, Magnesium, etc. All of that stuck as part of the Vraki language.  
  * They discovered that humans were spreading like rats, but they didn't seem to care, as they truly thought it wasn't a big deal at all.  
    * Some researchers who mattered, as they truly believed humans were powerless.  
  * \~1650 B.G. Naked Bruntos caused the Buhraies Plains East Dip to crumble, several wood people fell and slowly began to spread as well. Buhraies did not care, as that meant more trouble for Lishies and an opportunity to see how things would just work themselves out since Naked Bruntos can’t be that smart or powerful to cause back harm.  
    * Some of the Wood People became unchained.  
* **Bahyum Castle Period (\~1,500 B.G \- \~855 B.G.)**  
  * They built the biggest research station ever, called Bahyum, where they attempted to create an automated army factory, but they struggled to control it—it always had a life of its own.  
  * \~855 B.G. With a coordinated attack from the Lishies, Naked Bruntos, and a sudden turn for the worse within the Bahyum castle (sabotage), the Buhraies were in a pinch. However, they were too stubborn to fight together, and they began to lose ground against Naked Bruntos and Lishins.  
  * \~855 B.G. The Buhraies activated their last defenses by unchaining Wood People, but they mindlessly attacked everyone and everything.  
    * \*Unchaining as in removing the Fost Stone from the top of the Wood People  
    * Over the years, it rapidly multiplied, and as it expanded, it killed countless humans. However, humans figured out how to use the Fost Stone to their advantage and built villages with Wood People surrounding it.  
  * The Buhraies realized that the Wood People & Humans had basically taken over the land.  
    * They used up their remaining strength to launch a spell against the moon itself to make all living things visible, since humans were the most hidden and bundled together at night time, but the spell failed because it was not strong enough.  
    * Completely drained of their soul energy, Buhraies gave up and fled West, where Flatland now is located. Some Lishies came to this same spot as well, and a war occurred between them, leaving measles numbers on both sides until they created a ceasefire with the dwindling numbers.  
    * Due to their small numbers, the Buhraies formed a cooperation pact with them in exchange for sucking their blood to mount an assault to retake their plain—which never worked, as they were always on the defensive due to their lack of numbers.

* **New Home Period (\~855 B.G \- \~500 B.G.)**  
  * The Buhraies found themselves in what is now Flatland. The resources were scarce, which meant the Lishies did not care for it, but Lishies began to appear as humans toppled their cities, especially after \~805 B.G., with the fall of Aldemeri.  
    * They began to interbreed, and Darfaries, Wozefaries, & Sorefaries began to be born.  
  * \~500 B.G. The remnants of Buhraies & Lishies formed a pact with the Fuhraies.  
    * The intensity of the Naked Brontus attacks was still increasing exponentially against what is now Flatland. They were not successful in taking over the land, but at the same time, the Buhraies drained most of their Soul Energy power, and several of the Lishies were dying from having all their blood sucked out of them just to keep defending from human attacks on Flatland.  
    * The Fuhraies were mortified with their strength, and did not think twice about getting as far West as they could after the Buhraies proposed building a large mountain.  
* **Mountain Pact Period (\~500 B.G \- \~250 A.G.)**  
  * The mountain pact was developed, and a new expression to go alongside it:  
    * Mountain Greeting: Hands up, touching noses (if you can), stare into each other’s eyes and shake bellies together.  
  * \~495 B.G. They all fled into the desert to build a mountain to protect themselves against what they call a plague of Naked Brontus.  
  * The remnants of Buhraies, Lishies, and Fuhraies were now sharing a single mountain, and ended up interbreeding\* with more and more Darfaries, Wozefaries & Sorefaries started to be born, and Vraki became the official language.  
    * \*Fuhraies were unable to interbreed with any other species due to all other species being fleshy in nature.  
    * Buhraies developed the Darfaries interface during this period in an attempt to control the Darfaries as a foot soldier.  
  * \~97 A.G. They defended against an assault from Naked Bruntos.  
* **Expulsion Period (\~250 A.G \- \~270 A.G.)**  
  * \~250 A.G. The remnants of Buhraies tried a coup, but it failed. Lishies then tried as well, but their very few numbers meant the Darfaries had a leg up in defending themselves.  
  * Darfaries wiped out all the other species in the mountain. Some kept on fleeing West, but all likely met their demise.  
* **Sundeath Period (\~270 A.G \- \~380 A.G.)**  
  * A group of ancient species gathered and created a secondary mountain now known as Sundeath. They summoned Naked Bruntos again, but they soon rebelled and wiped out the ancient species with the help of other humans, who taught them Vraki and about the world.

# **\--Humans--**

  * Several humans were brought to the island of Buhraies Plains by Buhraies and taught Vraki and other research-related skills. They stayed in the middle islands alongside other scientific studies.

    * They blended themselves using tree leaves and covered themselves with dirt. They stole food from Buhraies' homes as they spread like rats.  
    * Buhraies knew about it, but they did not take action—they were more curious than anything.  
* **Tribal Period (\~1,900 B.G \- \~1,500 B.G.)**  
  * Wingas became their friends, allowing communication between humans, while Big Wingas were more often than not dangerous and territorial.  
  * They began seeking ways out of the Buhraies Plains and eventually found the Buhraies Plains East Dip, which was growing unstable.  
  * \~1650 B.G. They provoked a massive landfall and used it to escape in droves.  
  * Tribes formed based around the leftover recollections they had of themselves on the Buhraies plains.  
    * Also, in the lower plains, as villages spread around.  
  * Lishies began hunting humans.

  * Lishies began breeding and fattening humans by the thousands for food, and with such a large quantity of humans, some were always escaping.  
  * The Human population exploded all over the lower terrains.

    * Once a person’s heart is replaced with the stone, they can only feel the emotion set in it. Most who tried it ended up dying for various reasons, such as going berserk or taking their own lives. The few who were capable of living with the stone rose up to the legends.  
      * The stone also seeps into a person’s soul, corrupting it and causing many symptoms, including the inability to be revived.  
* **The Hazing of Aldemeri Period (\~900 B.G \- \~805 B.G.)**  
  * During this time, humans began striking back at Lishies by pitting them against one another.  
  * They were also capable of promoting a war with Lishin against Buhraies, while killing them, and sabotaging Bahyum castle.  
  * With the power of the stones, humans began working directly and indirectly through the shadow to set Aldemeri to fall.  
  * \~855 B.G. Buhraies unchained Wood People, which decimated most humans in the plains, but did not hinder human activity in the south plains.  
  * \~805 B.G. Aldemeri fell.  
* **The Hazing of Lishin Period (\~805 B.G \- \~525 B.G.)**  
  * With Aldemeri out of the picture, humans began focusing on Lishin, as it was the central location for attacking any other civilization.  
    * They failed to trigger another civil war, but they were able to promote an all-out war between Ganyrami and Lishin before taking over the remnants with their own war.  
  * \~525 B.G. Lishin fell.  
* **The Hazing of Ganyrami Period (\~525 B.G \- 0 B.G.)**  
  * \~495 B.G. Flatland was taken over by humans as the ancient species fled into the desert.  
  * \~438 B.G. Nari stones were discovered, which aided in the fight as a Soul Energy replacement  
  * \~338 B.G. Nari Gels were discovered, but mostly for poisoning enemies rather than healing.  
  * \~0 B.G Ganyrami fell. The Lower & Buhraies plains were now under human control.

## **After Ganyrami Time (0 \- 939 A.G.)**

* **The Indulgence Period (0 \- 120 A.G.)**  
  * With the fall of Ganyrami and the desert offering few resources, given how treacherous it is, humans slowed their attacks and shifted more toward daily life.  
  * A brief period in time where the human population was so sparse that conflicts were rare.  
    * The use of the Walking Heart stone began to fade as times grew peaceful.  
  * Some human settlements began to become villages, then cities.  
  * With an increase in population, bandits started to show up, and so Sanbi & Tolerideme Kingdoms formed from mighty adventurers (Not the same as Forway adventurers) that protected them.  
    * \*Both at this point cities instead of villages.  
  * Roads interconnecting these places began to be built. Including one of the first roads that goes up on the Buhraies Plains South Dip into Tolerideme.  
  * Rumors about ancient species look-alikes began to float around.  
    * At that time, the Gandew Kingdom was killing any Humans who dared to wander too close to the mountain.  
    * \~97 A.G. Humans mounted an assault, but the mountain was so protected that they gave up.  
  * Kingdoms began competing with each other.  
* **The Greed Period (120 \- 680 A.G.)**  
  * The sons and daughters of these adventures were born into a life of luxury and serenity at home, so they became greedy for even more, and killing over it was no big deal.  
  * Wars between the two Kingdoms started.  
    * The Walking Heart stones started to be used once again, especially within the armies.  
    * Carnage all over.  
  * \~150 A.G. Whiteland was discovered.  
  * 270\~380 A.G Humans helped free Sundeath from the ancient species. It also became a sort of refuge from all the skirmishes between human kingdoms.  
    * Wozefaries & Sorefaries slowly began to spread through the kingdoms, albeit with many being racist against them. Most were slaves. Some began to change public opinion by using their abilities and Soul Energy to help.  
  * \~420 A.G. Newland was discovered.  
  * \~470 A.G a previous Sanbi King decided to build a city on the Waterfall, which was a small village at this point, as a war measure against Tolerideme by flooding them, which did somewhat work.  
  * \~490 A.G. Old Fuhrair became a mining town.  
  * In 570 A. D., the Kingdom of Tolerideme was winning the war against Sanbi, and so it decided to march on Gandew, and a great war between the three kingdoms began.  
    * Flatland became a resting spot for the soldiers, especially to exchange information.  
    * Darfaries now were as flexible as Humans when it came to warfare, so it was not as easy.  
    * One after another, wars keep happening, but mostly end in a stalemate.  
  * \~570 A.G. EndlessFog & Triangle City expanded with all the people fleeing the wars. Same for what is now the Freeish Kingdom.  
    * Blood City was an offshoot where a village with tons of hunters existed.  
    * Deltacross grew from the constant flux of people traveling towards Freeish.  
  * In 572 A.G., Tolerideme exploded the road up Buhraies Plains South Dip after losing too many men in the war against Sanbi.  
  * 575 A.G., on a dark, cloudy night, a few people saw a person in robe-like clothing drunkenly walk around Tastyleaf, and a guard saw the person leave towards the swamp. The guard said it was dangerous, but the person replied, “I have a literal awesome fucking idea,” and kept walking.  
    * Cruros showed up soon after.  
  * In 640 A.G., an incredibly smart man named Freeish Paulin felt compelled to change the situation, so he began creating a secret base in what is now ForWay city to overthrow the kingdoms.  
  * In 648 A.G., Freeish Paulin was betrayed and sold to the Gandew Kingdom for assassination. However, as the king of the Gandew Kingdom learned what he was trying to do, he began to agree with most of it, including the overthrow of the current kingdoms. The main reason is that he realized that everyone would end up dead at the end of the day, and nothing would be solved.  
    * He still had to kill the adventurer to save face with the population, but he then began building connections and establishing a new base in what is now the Freeish Kingdom, as Paulin gave him all his connections.  
    * The king entrusted one of his closest allies (and mistresses) to spearhead the organization and granted her the position of Courtroom Scientist in the guise of “better war preparations.”  
  * In 650 A.G., the courtroom scientists of Gandew, Tolerideme, & Sanbi Kingdoms meet on this hidden base to discuss an overthrow plan.  
    * Gandew: Asa the Powerful  
    * Tolerideme: Adrian From North Tolerideme  
    * Sanbi: Bijura Moacir  
    * They slowly conditioned the populace to accept what they were trying to do in order to instigate revolts.  
      * “The greed of the heroes was controlling the people”  
    * The kingdoms gradually lost their grip on the populace until they could no longer wage war against one another. At that point, the rebellion truly started and toppled all three kingdoms.  
      * Gandew King was executed in front of everyone. He had a calm, relieved demeanor despite what had happened to him. Some people don’t believe he was that kind and selfless.  
      * Tolerideme King was starved to death.  
      * Sanbi King was humiliated by being publicly spanked, then dropped alive in the ocean.  
* **The Prosperity Treaty Period (680 A.G. \- Current)**  
  * The overthrow plan succeeded, and the courtroom scientists became the new kings.  
  * They crafted the Prosperity Treaty and Forway law to ensure that no more wars would occur over greed.  
  * They created a common currency, formalized a calendar that began in the year Ganyrami fell, and introduced other tools to bring everyone onto the same page and make trade easier.  
    * Adrian helped spur both the Calendar & UOMs.  
  * Embassies were founded soon after, and all ruins/major cities became property of Forway.  
    * Sundeath tried to stay out of everything, but failed due to the sheer pressure from Forway. In exchange, they have Pharaohs that serve as city leaders.
