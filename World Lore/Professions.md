# **Historians/Teachers**

* **Isekai History**  
  * Where humans live, history soon follows, filling libraries.  
  * However, some of history was lost during the greed period, when wars raged for a long time. Also, tons of history was lost because before Ganyrami, it was pretty much word of mouth among humans.  
    * Darfaries burned their own history, which makes the past kinda sketchy.  
  * However, some people don’t really care about history because they prefer living in the present. With that, people in this profession usually teach at the fundamental level to earn a livable wage while they pursue research that nobody is funding.

* **Skill Cost**  
  * Fundamental School  
  * Historian/Teaching School
