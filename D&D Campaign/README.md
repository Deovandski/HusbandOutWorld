# Welcome to the Alpha version of the **#HusbandOutWorld** campaign

The campaign involves **2–6 players** working as adventurers protecting a wagon from one place to another. More of a one‑shot type of campaign, but a long‑term campaign could definitely be set up.

---

## Daily Structure

Each day is split into chunks:

### **Graveyard (12am–6am)**
- Players decide who stays awake, if anyone.
- DM rolls dice for events.

### **Morning (6am–12pm)**
- Players decide their actions.
- DM rolls dice for events (or not).

### **Afternoon (12pm–6pm)**
- Players decide their actions.
- DM rolls dice for events (or not).

### **Night (6pm–12am)**
- Players decide their actions.
- DM rolls dice for events.

---

## Player Decisions

Players can create their own characters, or use an existing adventurer. See Adventurers.md within the same folder for further details!

For each time chunk, players decide:

1. **Stay for rest** — repair, hunt for good dinner, barter, flirt, steal, etc.  
   If in town or staying with other wagons, social actions become available. Prep Nari gels, etc.

2. **Travel pace** — push the Eguas harder to get there faster, or slow down and take the travel easier.  
   Use companions (if they have them) to check for danger. Let the Egua graze, etc.

3. **Roles** — who stays awake, who actively protects, who rests.

4. **Combat** — everyone takes turns based on their AC.

---

## DM Decisions

1. Events, enemies for event types, etc. Possible events:
###### *Ambush from Fauna or beast. Bagarisa swarm, etc
###### *Ambush from bandits
###### *Find corpses (adventurers, other wagon, etc.)
###### *Broken wagon.
###### *Nothing.

2. Rewards.  
3. Punishment (if caught by guards or reported by one of the people in the wagon or someone else.)

## Egua Food & Travel

**Food:**  
Primarily ~20 kg of Grass, Hay, or Carbey a day.  
Can graze on most grounds.  
Assume grazing every morning and night, so travel happens in the afternoon.

### **Movement Speeds**

- **Gallop (~30 km/h)**  
  - 5 km @ ~10% Load (Max Load ~90 kg)

- **Canter (~20 km/h)**  
  - 15 km @ ~10% Load (Max Load ~90 kg)  
  - 5 km @ ~20% Load (Max Load ~180 kg)

- **Trot (~10 km/h)**  
  - 35 km @ ~10% Load (Max Load ~90 kg)  
  - 15 km @ ~20% Load (Max Load ~180 kg)

- **Gait (~7 km/h)**  
  - 65 km @ ~10% Load (Max Load ~90 kg)  
  - 35 km @ ~20% Load (Max Load ~180 kg)

---

## Travel Time Example

6 hours of gaiting = **42 km/day**  
→ ~22 days  
→ 88 shifts  
→ Assuming ~5 minutes of playtime per shift 
→ ~7 hours of playtime

DM can tweak the numbers to make playtime faster and reduce the number of shifts.
