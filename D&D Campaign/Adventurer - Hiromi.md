# Hiromi — Level 3 Rogue (Inquisitor)
**Race:** Human (Darfaries‑adjacent)  
**Class:** Rogue (Inquisitor‑flavored Thief)  
**Background:** Royal Runaway  
**Alignment:** Lawful Neutral  

**AC:** 14  
**HP:** 24  
**Speed:** 30 ft  

**STR 10**  
**DEX 16**  
**CON 12**  
**INT 14**  
**WIS 13**  
**CHA 11**

---

### Features & Traits
**Reserved Politeness.** Advantage on Persuasion checks when calm; disadvantage when stressed.  
**Interrogator’s Edge.** Proficiency with torture tools; +2 to Intimidation.  
**Darfaries Mystery.** Immune to charm for 1 round (1/day).  
**Conflict Avoidant.** If an ally argues, Hiromi must make a DC 10 Wis save or leave the party for 1 round.

---

### Rogue Features
**Sneak Attack (2d6).**  
**Cunning Action.** Dash, Disengage, or Hide as bonus action.

---

### Actions
**Dagger.** +5 to hit, 1d4+3 piercing.  
**Shortbow.** +5 to hit, 1d6+3 piercing.  
**Clip & Question (Recharge 6).** Target must make DC 12 Wis save or be frightened for 1 round.

---

### Skills
Stealth +6, Investigation +6, Insight +4, Intimidation +4

---

### Equipment
Finger‑clipping tool, dark cloak, royal‑quality boots, hidden letters from home.
