### Features & Traits
**Narcolepsy.** When rolling initiative, roll a d6. On a 1, Magi starts the fight asleep until an ally wakes her.  
**Hunter’s Instinct.** Advantage on Survival checks to track.  
**Distrustful.** Disadvantage on Persuasion checks with strangers.  
**Wozefaries Blood.** Resistance to cold damage.
