# Harry Sanbi — Level 3 Soul‑Touched Wanderer
**Race:** Human–Bagarisa  
**Class:** Soul‑Touched (Homebrew Ranger/Monk hybrid)  
**Background:** Isekai Stranger  
**Alignment:** Neutral Good  

**AC:** 13  
**HP:** 24  
**Speed:** 30 ft  

**STR 12**  
**DEX 14**  
**CON 13**  
**INT 10**  
**WIS 11**  
**CHA 15**

---

### Features & Traits
**Bagarisa Soul Battery.** Regain 1 Soul Point per long rest.  
**Confused Memories.** Disadvantage on History checks about the world.  
**Claustrophobic.** When in tight spaces, DC 10 Wis save or frightened 1 round.  
**Adaptable Heart.** Advantage on one death save per long rest.  
**Curious Cook.** Can prepare meals granting +1 temp HP.

---

### Actions
**Unarmed Strike.** +4 to hit, 1d6+2 bludgeoning.  
**Improvised Weapon.** +4 to hit, 1d8+2.  
**Heart Pulse (Recharge 6).** 2d6 thunder, 10‑ft cone, Con save DC 11 half.

---

### Soul‑Energy Abilities
**Soul Flicker.** Bonus action: +10 ft movement for 1 round (2/day).  
**Soul Push.** Once per long rest: shove a creature 5 ft without a check.

---

### Skills
Persuasion +4, Insight +3, Survival +2, Perception +2

---

### Equipment
Traveler’s clothes, cooking kit, simple blade, notebook of fragmented memories.
